About NeuroScope

NeuroScope is an Augmented Reality app that helps users visualize and understand the human brain. Designed especially for educational and awareness purposes, it displays 3D brain models with automatic labels and informative descriptions.

Features

  • Real-time AR brain model visualization
  • Auto-labeling of major brain parts
  • Descriptions facing the camera
  • Beginner-friendly UI and design
